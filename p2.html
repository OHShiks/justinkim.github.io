1. Aftermath

1. What is a good working definition of what a System is and perhaps what it is not?

A system is a family comprised of interacting and interdependent parts or groups that come together to form a whole.

A system is not just one thing; it is comprised of multiple parts (joined together in a meaningful relationship) that come together to create a whole. A system is a family. A thing by itself is not a system. Systems may also be "boundary-less." There isn't necessarily an "end goal" or final solution.
